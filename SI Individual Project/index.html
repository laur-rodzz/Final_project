A Closer Look at "FAMJAM4000"

"FAMJAM4000" by ​Jordan Ward is a song that revolves around the theme of family and the importance of maintaining relationships with family members. The lyrics suggest that despite disagreements and misunderstandings, family is home and nothing else can replace it. The verses touch upon the narrator's personal experiences with family members, including his mother and an older relative who may have struggled with alcoholism. Through these experiences, the narrator acknowledges the challenges and conflicts that may arise within families but emphasizes the need to overcome them through communication and love. The lyrics suggest that family is important not just for the present but also for the future, as the narrator wants his children to have a relationship with their relatives and witness their growth.

A Closer Look at "Irrational"

"Irrational" by ​Shay Lia is a song that explores the complexities of romantic relationships and the irrational emotions that can arise within them. The lyrics hint at a tumultuous connection, where the narrator is deeply infatuated with someone but acknowledges that their love may not be reciprocated or healthy. The song describes the intense highs and lows of being caught up in this irrational love, with lyrics that touch upon feelings of obsession, desire, and moments of vulnerability.

A Closer Look at "Always"

"Always" by ​Daniel Caesar is a heartfelt song that explores the theme of unconditional love and commitment in a romantic relationship. The lyrics convey a deep sense of devotion and the desire to be there for a loved one through thick and thin. The song expresses the longing to offer unwavering support, understanding, and reassurance to a partner.

A Closer Look at "Can't Wait"

The song "Can't Wait" by ​Doja Cat expresses anticipation and longing for a specific person. The lyrics convey a deep connection and infatuation with this individual, highlighting the excitement and eagerness to be close to them. The chorus repeats the line "I can't wait to get next to you," emphasizing the singer's strong desire and impatience.

Artist Background

Jordan Ward

​Jordan Ward is a multifaceted artist who has made a name for himself in both the dance and music industries. Hailing from ​St. Louis, he began his artistic journey as a dancer, touring with renowned performers such as ​Janet Jackson, ​Beyonce, and ​Justin Bieber. However, Ward's passion for singing led him to explore his musical talents further. In 2017, he debuted his first EP, "A Peak at the Summit," followed by his first full-length album, "Valley Hopefuls." His latest EP, "Remain Calm," showcases his growth as an artist and features a blend of lighthearted tunes and introspective tracks. Ward's unique sound is heavily influenced by his gospel music upbringing and the vibrant musical theater community in St. Louis. With his innovative approach, Ward is carving out his own space in the music industry, bringing a new wave to the hip-hop genre.

Shay Lia

​Shay Lia, a ​French-Djiboutian musician, has captivated audiences with her soulful and melodic sound. Born Shanice Dileita Mohamed, she grew up in Djibouti before moving to ​Montreal, ​Canada. Exploring her diverse identity, Shay Lia blends R&B, funk, and soul influences into her music, often accompanied by infectious grooves and catchy melodies. Her signature sound has earned her collaborations with prominent artists like Kaytranada, including standout tracks like "Leave Me Alone" and "Chances." While rooted in her East African heritage and French roots, Shay Lia's music transcends borders, appealing to audiences worldwide. Her upbringing in Djibouti, her experiences as a multicultural artist, and her discovery of songwriting while studying at UQAM University have all contributed to the unique and mesmerizing artistry of Shay Lia. With her distinctive voice and creative vision, Shay Lia continues to make waves in the music industry.

Daniel Caesar

​Daniel Caesar is an incredibly talented ​Canadian ​R&B artist who has garnered widespread acclaim for his soulful and introspective music. Born as ​Ashton D. Simmonds in 1995, he adopted the stage name Daniel Caesar as a tribute to the biblical figures Daniel and Caesar. Caesar's signature sound blends elements of R&B, gospel, and soul, creating a musical experience that is both nostalgic and contemporary. His breakthrough single, "Get You," featuring Kali Uchis, received critical acclaim and established him as a rising star in the industry. Caesar released his debut album, "Freudian," in 2017, which was met with widespread praise and earned him several award nominations, including a Grammy. Known for his heartfelt songwriting and soothing vocals, Caesar explores themes of love, spirituality, and personal growth in his music. With his smooth, captivating voice and introspective lyrics, Daniel Caesar continues to captivate audiences around the world as one of R&B's most promising young artists.

Doja Cat

​Doja Cat, born ​Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini on October 21, 1995, is an influential ​American ​rapper, ​singer, and songwriter. She first soared to fame with her viral hit "Mooo!" in 2018, a playful track that showcased her dynamic versatility and unapologetic style. Her aptitude for blending quirky, idiosyncratic lyrics with catchy hooks quickly established her as a fresh voice in the music scene. Doja's discography deftly fuses elements of pop, hip hop, and R&B, setting her apart in a crowded industry. Her sophomore album, "Hot Pink," solidified her stardom with hits like "Say So," which not only dominated charts globally but also led to Grammy nominations. Her artistic persona is characterized by a chameleonic fashion sense and a bold online presence. Always evolving, Doja Cat continually pushes the envelope with her musical experimentation, daring visuals, and vibrant performances, cementing her status as a creative powerhouse.
